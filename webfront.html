How Intellicar Works

Intellicar combines advanced computer vision and sensor technology to monitor both the driver and the road environment in real-time.

1. Driver Monitoring

Continuously tracks the driver's face and eyes using an infrared camera to detect signs of drowsiness or distraction.

2. Distance Sensing

Uses ultrasonic sensors and cameras to measure the distance to objects in front of the vehicle in real-time.

3. AI Analysis

Advanced algorithms process the data to determine if the driver is drowsy or if a collision is imminent.

4. Alert System

Triggers audible beeps and visual warnings when drowsiness is detected or objects are too close.

Intellicar Safety Features

Drowsiness Detection

Advanced eye-tracking technology monitors blink patterns and eye closure duration to detect signs of driver fatigue.

  • Infrared camera works in all lighting conditions
  • Measures PERCLOS (percentage of eye closure)
  • Adapts to individual blinking patterns

Real-Time Distance Monitoring

Ultrasonic sensors and cameras continuously measure the distance to objects in front of your vehicle.

  • Works at speeds from 0-120 mph
  • Accurate to within 10cm
  • Functions in various weather conditions

Smart Alert System

Intelligently triggers audible and visual alerts based on the severity of the detected threat.

  • Progressive alert intensity
  • Customizable alert sounds
  • Haptic feedback option
